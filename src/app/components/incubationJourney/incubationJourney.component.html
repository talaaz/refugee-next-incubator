Your Journey

Entrepreneurship School → Accounting classes → Co-founder matching → Mentorship → Funding (6 months)

  1. Chapter One

    Manage your classes on your own time! All our classes are online, and are interactive for a better learning experience.

    1. Before: Dont have an idea yet? We will help you come up with one.
    2. Week 1: Turning your startup vision into a business plan: The Business Model Canvas
    3. Week 2: Market Validation (TAM, SAM, SOM)
    4. Week 3: Resource Mobilization
    5. Week 4: Building your prototype
    6. Week 5: Marketing
    7. Week 6: Pitching
    8. Week 7: The mindset of a successful entrepreneur
  2. Accounting (Online)

    Learn how to manage your cash flow, maintain a balance sheet, identifying a path to profitability and forecasting the future of your business.

  3. Co-founder matching

    We will help you find a Danish co-founder who is passionate about the same field as you and can help maneuver through the Danish system.

  4. Mentorship

    We will match your team with an experienced business mentor who has worked in your field. They can give you advice, support and network to help you along.

  5. Funding

    Helping you apply for soft funding and funding competitions.
